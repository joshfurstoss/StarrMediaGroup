02 — The Solution

Not a personal brand.
A media company
built on yours.

Starr Media Group is a fully staffed, revenue-linked media operation built on top of your portfolio. It is not content marketing. It is not a PR retainer. It is a demand generation engine structured as a media company — with Josh Furstoss as the operating partner and you as the face and talent. You show up. The machine runs.
01

The Flagship Show

Weekly long-form podcast and video. You interview your network — the investors, the defense contractors, the AI operators, the policy makers. Calls you are already having. We just put a camera on them. Distributed to YouTube, LinkedIn, Spotify, Apple Podcasts.

02

The Starr Report

Weekly newsletter. Opinionated, specific, impossible to ignore. Covers AI operationalization, enterprise infrastructure, and the tech-policy intersection. Grows to 15,000+ subscribers in 12 months. Sponsors pay $5K–$10K per issue at that scale.

03

The Content Engine

Every episode becomes 10 pieces of content — clips, posts, threads, blog entries — distributed across LinkedIn, X, and YouTube Shorts. Amdahl analyzes engagement signals and routes warm leads directly into CloudMind's sales process.

04

The Pipeline Layer

The media operation is the top of CloudMind's funnel. Every podcast guest is a potential customer or referral. Every newsletter subscriber is an ICP prospect. Media does not just build brand. It generates qualified pipeline with data behind it.
